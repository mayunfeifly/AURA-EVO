# AURA EVO

> Your digital twin. Evolve yourself.

AURA EVO is a Chrome extension that lets you distill your knowledge, style, and thinking into an AI-powered digital twin. Share your expertise, subscribe to others, and build a personal AI ecosystem.

## Product Vision

- **Personal Distillation**: Upload documents, conversations, and notes to create your AI twin
- **Expert Marketplace**: Discover and subscribe to expert AI twins in finance, tech, cuisine, and more
- **Creator Economy**: Share your twin, earn from subscriptions and usage
- **Self-Evolution**: Your twin learns and improves from every interaction

## Project Structure

```
└── README.md
```
